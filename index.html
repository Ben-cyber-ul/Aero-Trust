Frequently Asked Questions

Carry-on: one cabin bag (up to 7kg) and one personal item. Checked baggage depends on fare—typical allowances: Economy 20kg, Business 30kg, First 40kg. Exact allowances and fees are shown at booking; additional baggage can be purchased during checkout.

Changes and cancellations depend on the fare rules associated with your ticket. Refundable fares allow refunds (may take 7–14 business days); non-refundable fares may be eligible for travel credit minus fees. For demo bookings in this site, bookings are stored locally and can be edited or cleared from the Bookings section.

Yes — group discounts are typically available for parties of 10 or more. Contact our sales team at groups@aerotrust.com to request a quote and negotiate group fares.

Aero Trust follows strict safety standards including scheduled maintenance, certified flight crews, continuous operational monitoring, and compliance with regulatory requirements. We also train staff regularly on safety and emergency procedures.

Online check-in usually opens 48 hours before departure and closes 1 hour before. You can check in online and use a mobile boarding pass where available; otherwise collect your boarding pass at the airport check-in desk. Have a valid ID or passport and travel documents ready.

We accept major credit/debit cards and secure payment providers (e.g., Visa, Mastercard, and PayPal) depending on your country. All payments are processed over secure gateways and card details are not stored on this demo site.

Refunds are processed according to the fare conditions of your ticket. Refundable fares are eligible for refunds to the original payment method; non-refundable fares may be converted to travel credit less applicable fees. Please allow 7–14 business days for refund processing.

Small pets in cabin are allowed on selected routes for a fee and require advance notice and an approved carrier. Service animals are accepted with appropriate documentation. Contact our support team before booking to confirm pet policies for your specific route.

Please request assistance at least 48 hours before departure. You can add assistance requests during booking or contact our support team via bookings@aerotrust.com or the phone number on our Contact section.

Passengers are responsible for ensuring they have valid passports, visas, and any required health documents for their destination. Check embassy or government websites for up-to-date entry requirements before travel.
